#+title: left-inverse

An [[id:deb29391-b39d-41f4-aa9b-5840c873ba09][object]] $X$ in a [[id:06fc0ae5-533c-4b99-be3a-26b98dddae39][category]] is called a /*retract*/ of an object $Y$ if there
exist [[id:fca06db8-da2d-434a-888d-16190002dbf1][morphisms]] $s : X \to Y$ and $r : Y \to A$ such that $rs = 1X$, where $r$
is called a /*retraction*/ of $Y$ onto $X$ and $s$ is called a [[id:87aa130b-1cfe-45a2-9722-898fb32ef822][section]] of $r$.
