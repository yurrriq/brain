* An [[object]] $X$ in a [[category]] is called a /*retract*/ of an object $Y$ if there
exist [[morphisms]] $s : X \to Y$ and $r : Y \to A$ such that $rs = 1X$, where $r$
is called a /*retraction*/ of $Y$ onto $X$ and $s$ is called a [[section]] of $r$.
