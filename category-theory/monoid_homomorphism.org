#+title: monoid homomorphism

A [[id:055f644b-3f1e-484a-b4a7-f9bd854f9da0][homomorphism]] between [[id:d33f55e0-14a5-47e3-86d9-3f283bab59c9][monoids]] $A$ and $B$ consists of:
- a [[id:fb96672b-957d-4e11-a646-dd3c15607cf9][semigroup homomorphism]] $f : A \to B$ of the underlying [[id:867c8703-b7fd-4219-830c-634032823e01][semigroups]]

such that:
- $f$ respects the identity: $f(1_A) = 1_B)$
