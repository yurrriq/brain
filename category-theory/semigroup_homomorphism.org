#+title: semigroup homomorphism

A [[id:055f644b-3f1e-484a-b4a7-f9bd854f9da0][homomorphism]] between [[id:867c8703-b7fd-4219-830c-634032823e01][semigroups]] $A$ and $B$ consists of:
- a function $f : A \to B$ of the underlying sets

such that:
- $f$ respects $\cdot$: $f(x \cdot y) = f(x) \cdot f(y)$
