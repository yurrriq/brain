#+title: monoid

A monoid $(M, \cdot)$ consists of:
- a set $M$
- an [[id:4b5ec273-b6b0-4b78-b3a3-7e6560a8f7fa][associative]] binary operation $\cdot$
- an identity element $1$

satisfying the following [[id:eb05b428-9099-4308-b817-825c0f89c071][axioms]]:
 - *Associativity*: $(x \cdot y) \cdot z = x \cdot (y \cdot z)$
 - *Identity*: $1 \cdot x = x = x \cdot 1$
