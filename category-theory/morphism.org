#+title: morphism

A *morphism* is a structure-preserving *map* (or *arrow*) between [[id:deb29391-b39d-41f4-aa9b-5840c873ba09][objects]] of the
same type.

For example, $f : X \to Y$ is a *morphism* with [[id:15583214-dd47-426b-b1eb-86d92bfa565b][domain]] (or *source*) $X$ and
[[id:459a0131-65a1-4a2f-966b-a93556548037][codomain]] (or *target*) $Y$.
