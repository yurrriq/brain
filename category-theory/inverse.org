#+title: inverse

The /*inverse*/ of a [[id:fca06db8-da2d-434a-888d-16190002dbf1][morphism]] $f : X \to Y$ is another [[id:fca06db8-da2d-434a-888d-16190002dbf1][morphism]]
$f^{-1} : Y \to X$, such that $f^{-1}$ is a
- [[id:9f2ac780-945e-44ff-a2ee-e06004488c6a][left-inverse]] of $f$: $f^{-1} \circ f = 1_X$
- [[id:87aa130b-1cfe-45a2-9722-898fb32ef822][right-inverse]] of $f$: $f \circ f^{-1} = 1_Y$

A [[id:fca06db8-da2d-434a-888d-16190002dbf1][morphism]] that has an /*inverse*/ is called an [[id:79449373-1b4e-4539-a9c6-cf60e3706bc5][isomorphism]].
