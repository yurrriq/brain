* A /*cyclic group*/ is a quotient [[group]] of the additive [[group]] of integers.
* Example: \(C_7 = \braket{a : a^7 = 1}\)
** \(a^5 \cdot a^4 = a^2 \leftrightsquigarrow 5 + 4 \equiv 2 \bmod 7 \)
