#+title: hom-set

For $x$ and $y$ in a [[id:06fc0ae5-533c-4b99-be3a-26b98dddae39][category]] $\mathcal{C}$, the /*hom-set*/ or /*hom-class*/ is
the collection of all [[id:fca06db8-da2d-434a-888d-16190002dbf1][morphisms]] from $x$ to $y$, written as $\hom(x,y)$,
$\hom_{\mathcal{C}}(x,y)$, $Hom(x,y)$, $Hom_{\mathcal{C}}(x,y)$, $Mor(x,y)$,
$Mor_{\mathcal{C}}(x,y)$, or simply $\mathcal{C}(x,y)$.
